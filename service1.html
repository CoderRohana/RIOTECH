Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI-powered frontend development services

What technologies do you use for AI-powered development?

We utilize a combination of advanced AI tools including GPT-based code assistants, AI design generators, automated testing frameworks, and performance optimization algorithms. Our development stack includes HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), React.js, and modern CSS frameworks like Tailwind CSS.

How long does it take to complete a project?

Most standard projects are completed within 48 hours. Complex projects with additional features may require more time, which we will discuss during the requirement analysis phase. Our AI-assisted development process significantly reduces development time compared to traditional methods.

Do you provide source code after project completion?

Yes, you receive full ownership of all source code and assets upon project completion and payment. We deliver all files through your preferred method (Git repository, cloud storage, or direct download) with complete documentation.

What if I need changes after project delivery?

We offer 3 rounds of revisions during the development process. After project delivery, we provide lifetime support* with affordable rates for any future modifications or updates. Minor adjustments are often handled at no extra cost depending on the scope.

How do you ensure the website is responsive?

We use AI-powered testing tools that simulate hundreds of device types and screen sizes during development. Our responsive design approach uses flexible grids, responsive images, and CSS media queries to ensure perfect display on mobile phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops.

What is included in the lifetime support?

Lifetime support* includes assistance with deployment, guidance on content updates, and troubleshooting for issues related to our delivered code. It does not include major redesigns or adding new features, which are available at discounted rates for existing clients.
